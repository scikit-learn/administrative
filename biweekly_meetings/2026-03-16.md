### Performance and scaling — threading heuristics

New analysis across several platforms has not produced a clear, universal heuristic for optimal threading settings ([#30662](https://github.com/scikit-learn/scikit-learn/issues/30662)). Results vary significantly between Linux servers and local machines, even with similar system information. Alternatives under discussion include inspecting system information (e.g., cache size) via command-line tools, or providing a utility that runs a benchmark on the user's machine and stores a locally tuned heuristic.
