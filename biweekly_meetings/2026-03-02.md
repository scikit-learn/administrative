### Performance and scaling

Antoine is investigating the OpenMP threading performance slowdown
([#30662](https://github.com/scikit-learn/scikit-learn/issues/30662),
[#32955](https://github.com/scikit-learn/scikit-learn/pull/32955)). Results from
multiple platforms confirm that `fit` and `predict` are affected differently, so
separate solutions will be needed. Next step is to extend benchmarks to 32–64
physical cores (current data only covers up to 8) and to record performance vs.
efficiency core information for a clearer scaling picture.
